About

My Story

My name is Melissa, and I'm a UI/UX designer who calls Canada her home. However, I spent my formative years in Costa Rica, where I also attended culinary school before relocating to Toronto. The pandemic played a pivotal role in my discovery of UI/UX design, and it wasn't long before I fell in love with infusing existing systems with visually striking and user-friendly designs. I'm a naturally creative person, and my culinary background has allowed me to hone my problem-solving skills while expressing my creativity through cooking. UI/UX design is an ideal profession for me, as it allows me to combine my passion for creativity and problem-solving with a constantly evolving industry. I'm constantly learning and adapting to the latest trends, while keeping the user experience as my primary focus. Ultimately, my goal is to create designs that not only look fantastic but also provide users with an intuitive and enjoyable experience. So, if you're looking to collaborate with a passionate and dedicated UI/UX designer who's always ready to create something amazing, then look no further than me,
